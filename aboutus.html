Graduation Project

“VR Chemistry Lab” Grade: Excellent.


Chemistry Lab VR is an educational Virtual Reality experience that teaches students lab procedure and safety

Chemistry Lab VR is an educational experience that can virtually simulate lab procedures and important lab safety measures. The user is immediately immersed inside a Virtual Reality laboratory and can begin walking around using VR glasses to interact with the environment. There are lab procedures and safety guides spread across the tables, and a great deal of lab equipment that can be picked up, placed, thrown, or used in real lab procedures.

Why are we doing this project?

To facilitate students to do chemical experiments themselves and to identify chemical elements from inside the lab and using the tools without going to the lab, Whereas we already said the project is based on virtual reality (VR), in a realistic way using VR tools and technique to get over the limitations of doing chemical experiments From the absence of some chemicals and reduce the cost.
